# 🏥 Hospital Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Hospital Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The project focuses on analyzing hospital patient data to gain insights into patient demographics, treatment costs, hospital revenue, recovery performance, readmission trends, and patient satisfaction.

The dashboard helps healthcare administrators and decision-makers monitor key hospital performance indicators and identify areas for operational improvement through data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall hospital performance using key metrics.
* Monitor patient admission and treatment trends.
* Identify high-cost medical conditions and procedures.
* Evaluate patient recovery and readmission rates.
* Measure patient satisfaction levels.
* Support data-driven healthcare decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* CSV Dataset
* Data Visualization Techniques
* Business Intelligence & Analytics

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains patient-level hospital records with information related to:

| Field          | Description                      |
| -------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Patient ID     | Unique patient identifier        |
| Age            | Patient age                      |
| Gender         | Male/Female                      |
| Condition      | Medical condition diagnosed      |
| Procedure      | Treatment or procedure performed |
| Cost           | Treatment cost                   |
| Length of Stay | Number of hospitalization days   |
| Readmission    | Re-admission status              |
| Outcome        | Recovery status                  |
| Satisfaction   | Patient satisfaction score       |

---

# 📊 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard consists of **3 interactive pages**.

---

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of hospital performance through KPI cards and visual analytics.

### KPI Cards

* Total Patients
* Total Revenue
* Readmission Rate %
* Recovery Rate %
* Average Treatment Cost

### Visualizations

* Total Patients by Condition
* Cost by Condition
* Total Patients by Gender
* Cost vs Length of Stay

### Key Purpose

Provides a quick overview of patient volume, hospital revenue, recovery performance, and treatment costs.

---

## 2️⃣ Financial Analysis

This page focuses on treatment costs and revenue generation.

### KPI Cards

* Average Cost per Day
* Average Length of Stay
* Highest Cost Treatment
* Total Procedures

### Visualizations

* Revenue by Procedure
* Average Cost by Condition
* Average Length of Stay by Condition
* Procedure Distribution

### Key Purpose

Helps identify high-revenue procedures, expensive conditions, and cost-driving factors.

---

## 3️⃣ Risk Analysis

This page focuses on patient outcomes, readmissions, and satisfaction analysis.

### KPI Cards

* Readmission Count
* Average Patient Satisfaction

### Visualizations

* Outcome Distribution
* Readmission by Condition
* Readmission by Procedure
* Satisfaction by Condition

### Key Purpose

Identifies high-risk conditions and procedures while evaluating overall treatment effectiveness.

---

# 📈 Key Insights

* Certain medical conditions generate significantly higher treatment costs.
* Longer hospital stays generally lead to increased treatment expenses.
* Readmission rates vary across different conditions and procedures.
* Most patients recover successfully after treatment.
* Patient satisfaction differs depending on the condition and treatment received.
